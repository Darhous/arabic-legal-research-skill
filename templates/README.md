# Templates

This directory currently contains no DOCX template assets. It documents the future template requirements for Arabic legal academic research.

## Future Templates

- `colored-print.docx` - colored print style.
- `black-and-white-print.docx` - monochrome print style.
- `styles.json` or equivalent style settings file.

## Template Requirements

Future templates must support:

- A4 page size.
- Print-ready margins.
- Arabic RTL paragraphs and headings.
- Arabic academic fonts.
- Page borders.
- Centered bold headings.
- `مبحث` page breaks.
- Justified paragraphs.
- Paragraph indentation.
- Controlled line spacing.
- RTL footnotes and footer.
- Page numbers.
- Table of contents.
- Bibliography.

## Structural Invariance

Colored and black-and-white templates must preserve the same document structure. They may differ in color, borders, and decorative styling only.

## Validation Note

Future templates must be validated after generation or editing. Do not assume Microsoft Word field behavior, table of contents updates, or per-page footnote restart works until checked.
