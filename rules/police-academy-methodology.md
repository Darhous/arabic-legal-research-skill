# Police Academy Methodology Rules

These rules encode the first repository interpretation of **"الوجيز في مناهج البحث العلمي للدراسات القانونية والأمنية"**, prepared by Dr. Ibrahim El-Shereai, Police Academy, 2024.

## Definition of Scientific Research

Scientific research is organized research effort by a researcher to study a research problem using a scientific method, reaching results and recommendations under academic supervision.

## Three Pillars of Good Research

Good research depends on coherence between:

- الموضوع
- الشكل
- المنهج

The Skill must check all three. A paper with acceptable wording but weak problem definition, poor structure, or unsupported citations is not complete.

## Requirements of Good Research

The research should have:

- Clear title.
- Defined research problem.
- Clear objectives.
- Contribution to knowledge.
- Compatibility with the researcher’s capacity.
- Sufficient time.
- Objectivity and absence of bias.
- Proper writing style.
- Academic honesty in quotation.
- Available sources and references.
- Coherence between research parts.

## Researcher Qualities Reflected in Output

The produced paper should reflect:

- Objectivity and independence from personal bias.
- Desire to conduct scientific research.
- Broad-mindedness and freedom from bias and rigidity.
- Knowledge of libraries, especially electronic libraries.
- Commitment to research principles.
- Academic honesty when quoting and attributing ideas.
- Analytical, critical, writing, and discussion skills.
- Avoidance of rushing in a way that damages research results.

## Title Requirements

The research title must be:

- Indicative and expressive of the subject.
- Modern.
- Comprehensive.
- Accurate and clear.
- Attractive without exaggeration.
- Concise.
- Not contrary to public order.
- Not contrary to law.
- Not based on rhyme or poetic musicality.
- Not in question form.
- Methodologically connected to all research headings.
- Not changed after approval except with supervisor approval.

## Methodological Introduction Requirements

The methodological introduction must include:

- تمهيد
- مشكلة الدراسة
- أهمية الدراسة
- الدراسات السابقة
- أهداف الدراسة
- نوع ومنهج الدراسة
- حدود الدراسة
- فروض الدراسة
- تساؤلات الدراسة
- أدوات الدراسة
- مفاهيم الدراسة إن وجدت
- صعوبات الدراسة إن وجدت
- خطة الدراسة

Each item must connect to the title and research problem. Do not include decorative methodology sections that add no analytical value.

## Research Plan Quality

If no approved plan is supplied, a proposed plan should be:

- Modern.
- Non-imitative.
- Covering the research title.
- Covering all issues raised by the topic.
- Balanced quantitatively and qualitatively.
- Coherent from introduction to conclusion.

If an approved plan is supplied, preserve it exactly unless the user explicitly requests a change.

## Methodology Failure Conditions

Fail methodology review if:

- The title is vague, rhetorical, illegal, or in question form.
- The research problem is missing or disconnected from the title.
- Objectives are generic or unrelated to the problem.
- The body plan does not cover the topic.
- The conclusion introduces unsupported findings.
- Results and recommendations are not derived from the research body.
- Citation behavior violates academic honesty.
