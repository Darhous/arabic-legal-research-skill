# Methodology Review Checklist

Use this checklist to review compliance with the Police Academy methodology rules.

## Title

- [ ] Title is indicative and expressive.
- [ ] Title is modern.
- [ ] Title is comprehensive enough for the topic.
- [ ] Title is accurate and clear.
- [ ] Title is concise.
- [ ] Title is not in question form.
- [ ] Title does not rely on rhyme or poetic musicality.
- [ ] Title is not contrary to law or public order.
- [ ] Title connects to all research headings.

## Research Problem

- [ ] Research problem is explicitly stated.
- [ ] Problem is legal and academically researchable.
- [ ] Problem is not merely a topic label.
- [ ] Problem connects to objectives and questions.

## Introduction Components

- [ ] تمهيد
- [ ] مشكلة الدراسة
- [ ] أهمية الدراسة
- [ ] الدراسات السابقة
- [ ] أهداف الدراسة
- [ ] نوع ومنهج الدراسة
- [ ] حدود الدراسة
- [ ] فروض الدراسة
- [ ] تساؤلات الدراسة
- [ ] أدوات الدراسة
- [ ] مفاهيم الدراسة إن وجدت
- [ ] صعوبات الدراسة إن وجدت
- [ ] خطة الدراسة

## Plan Quality

- [ ] Approved plan is preserved if supplied.
- [ ] Proposed plan is modern if no approved plan exists.
- [ ] Plan covers the research title.
- [ ] Plan covers the research problem.
- [ ] Plan is balanced quantitatively.
- [ ] Plan is balanced qualitatively.

## Academic Integrity

- [ ] Quotations are academically honest.
- [ ] Sources are available or marked for verification.
- [ ] Analysis is objective.
- [ ] Results and recommendations follow from the body.
